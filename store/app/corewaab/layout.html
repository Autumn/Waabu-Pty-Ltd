Questions?
How much bandwidth? We currently offer 200GB monthly bandwidth on all Waab subscriptions.
What can I do on a Waab? Everything! We provide you with root administrator access to each Waab. The only software that runs on a Waab is your software.
How am I billed? Waabs are priced at a monthly rate, per month or per year.
Am I billed while my Waab is powered off? Yes. Your CPU, RAM, storage and network resources are all reserved for you during your subscription.
Which plan should I choose? If you're unsure, start with our BaseWaab and work up from there.
